March for Jesus Belfast 2026 Expected to Exceed Last Year with 16,000–20,000 Attendees; John Ahern Leads Growing Movement

March for Jesus Belfast is on track for significant growth, with organisers expecting 16,000–20,000 participants on Saturday 16 May 2026, following the success of 8,000+ attendees in Belfast and 25,000 in Dublin in 2025.

March for Jesus UK and Ireland has announced that this year's March for Jesus Belfast is expected to attract substantially more participants than the inaugural event last year. The growing momentum reflects increased church involvement, broader community participation, and a groundswell of support for public Christian witness across Ireland.

John Ahern, organiser of March for Jesus Belfast and coordinator of March for Jesus across Europe, said: "Last year we brought together over 8,000 people here in Belfast and 25,000 in Dublin. The response has been extraordinary. This year, we're preparing for significant growth as more churches, communities, and individuals recognise the power of unified Christian public testimony."

"March for Jesus is about exalting the Name of Jesus Christ in the heart of our cities. It's non-political, family-friendly, and rooted in worship, prayer, and hope. As we face unprecedented spiritual and social challenges, Christians from every denomination are uniting to declare our faith publicly and peacefully. The movement is growing across Europe and the world—Belfast is part of a much larger story," John explained.

2026 Expansion:

• Expected attendance: 16,000–20,000 (up from 8,000+ in 2025)
• European coordination: John Ahern leads March for Jesus across multiple European nations
• Multi-denominational participation: Churches and Christian groups from across Ireland
• Family-friendly focus: Entire families, youth groups, and individuals welcome

Event Details — Saturday 16 May 2026:
10:00 AM–2:00 PM: Ormeau Park Gathering (opposite Ozone Complex)
2:00 PM–2:50 PM: March through Belfast City Centre
3:00 PM–5:00 PM: Worship and prayer at Belfast City Hall

March for Jesus Belfast is free to attend and open to all ages and denominations. Registration is encouraged via Eventbrite to assist with planning. For full details, visit marchforjesus.co.uk.
