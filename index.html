About the March

March for Jesus Belfast is a family-friendly, non-political march celebrating Jesus through the streets of Belfast.

We are part of a worldwide movement, with marches taking place across 16+ European countries and many more around the globe. Together, we unite in worship, prayer, and testimony as we publicly declare our faith.

People from all churches and denominations come together for this joyful celebration — bringing banners, flags, music, and praise as we march through the heart of Belfast.

Everyone is welcome to join us. Whether you've marched before or this is your first time, come and be part of something bigger.
